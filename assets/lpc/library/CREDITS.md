## Downloaded Assets

This library contains a curated subset of the full LPC spritesheet collection,
organized for use as a paper-doll character system in the Teramor game.

Categories included:
- Body base layers (male, female, muscular, skeleton, zombie, teen)
- Head layers (human m/f, orc, goblin, skeleton, zombie, lizard, troll, vampire, wolf)
- Facial features (eyes, eyebrows, nose, beard, mustache)
- Hair styles (26+ styles with color variants)
- Torso clothing (29+ options: longsleeve, tunic, shirt, vest, robe, etc.)
- Armor (plate, leather, chainmail for torso/legs/feet/arms)
- Legs (11 types: pants, pantaloons, skirts, armor greaves, etc.)
- Feet (10 types: boots, shoes, sandals, plate armor, etc.)
- Hats/Helmets (138+ options across cloth, magic, pirate, helmet categories)
- Capes, Shoulders, Arms (bracers, gloves)
- Weapons (sword, ranged, polearm, blunt, magic)
- Shields (crusader, kite, heater)
- Accessories (backpack, quiver, dress)
